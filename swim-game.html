A hungry shark is chasing you through 5 danger zones! Dodge obstacles, collect treasures, and use your special ability to survive!

📖 How to Play & Object Guide

🎮 Controls

↑ / W — Swim Up  ·  ↓ / S — Swim Down  ·  Space / ⚡ button — Special Ability

⚠️ Avoid These

🪨 🪵 🪼
Obstacles — Costs 20 HP and lets the chaser gain on you!
🦈
The Chaser — Speeds up each zone. Caught = Game Over!

⭐ Collect These

🌟 💎 🪙 🐚
Treasures — +10 score & push the chaser back!

🎁 Power-Ups (rare glowing items)

⚡
Speed Boost — Swim 1.85× faster for 5 s.
🛡️
Shield — Blocks the next obstacle hit completely.
💊
HP Kit — Restores 30 HP.
🧲
Magnet — Auto-collects nearby treasures for 6 s.

🌊 The 5 Zones

🏖️
Shallow Waters (0 m) — Calm start.
🪸
Coral Reef (200 m) — Faster pace, more obstacles.
🌆
Twilight Zone (500 m) — Darker, explosive obstacles!
🌙
Midnight Zone (900 m) — Very fast. Bombs appear!
🕳️
The Abyss (1 400 m) — Maximum chaos. Good luck!

⚡ Special Abilities (Press Space)

🏊
Swimmer — Sprint: Doubles speed for 3.5 s. (8 s cooldown)
🐬
Dolphin — Sonar: Clears ALL obstacles! (10 s cooldown)
🦈
Shark — Roar: Pushes chaser far back! (9 s cooldown)
🐢
Turtle — Shell: Full invincibility for 4 s! (12 s cooldown)
