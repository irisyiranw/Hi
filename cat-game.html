🐱 Cat Chase

A dog is hot on your tail through 5 city zones! Dodge obstacles, collect treats, and use your special ability to escape!

Choose Your Cat

📖 How to Play & Object Guide

🎮 Controls

↑ / W — Run Up  ·  ↓ / S — Run Down  ·  Space / ⚡ button — Special Ability

⚠️ Avoid These

🐶 🧱 🗑️
Obstacles — Costs 20 HP and lets the chaser gain on you!
🐕
The Dog — Speeds up each zone. Caught = Game Over!

⭐ Collect These

🐟 🧶 🎀 🐾
Treats — +10 score & push the dog back!

🎁 Power-Ups (rare glowing items)

⚡
Speed Boost — Run 1.85× faster for 5 s.
🛡️
Shield — Blocks the next obstacle hit completely.
💊
HP Kit — Restores 30 HP.
🧲
Magnet — Auto-collects nearby treats for 6 s.

🏙️ The 5 Zones

🏡
Backyard (0 m) — Calm start.
🏘️
Neighborhood (200 m) — Faster pace, more obstacles.
🏪
Downtown (500 m) — Busy streets, traffic cones!
🚗
Highway (900 m) — Very fast. Watch out!
🌆
City Center (1400 m) — Maximum chaos. Good luck!

⚡ Special Abilities (Press Space)

🐱
Tabby — Pounce: Doubles speed for 3.5 s. (8 s cooldown)
🐈‍⬛
Black Cat — Shadow: Full invincibility for 4 s! (12 s cooldown)
🐯
Tiger Cat — Roar: Pushes dog far back! (9 s cooldown)
🦁
Lion Cat — Pride Roar: Clears ALL obstacles! (10 s cooldown)
